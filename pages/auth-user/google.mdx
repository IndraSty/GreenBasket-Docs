# Login With Google

Login using your google account

### Endpoint

```md filename="Base URL"
http://localhost:8080
```

```md filename="Method and Endpoint"
DELETE /api/auth/{provider}
```

```md filename="Headers"
Authorization: Bearer access_token
```
### Route Param

`provider` : string (required, `google`)

### Responses
You will redirect to google sign in, and then You can choose Your google account to login/signup
